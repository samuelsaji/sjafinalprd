About Us

Started in 2010, SJA has been creating work characterized by a sensitivity to place, context and user identities. Winner of various awards including the IA&B National Young Designer of the year award (2012) and 4 IIA Kerala Chapter Awards. They were selected among the 15 architects from India to take part in the Bangalore workshop in 2015 at IIM Bangalore. 12 years, 6 awards, 200+ projects, 40+ alumni. SJA has been making architecture that belongs to its context, and creating pure spaces of well being for people.
