Vimal-Laxmi Residence

Project Area :2600 SQFT
Site Area : 278 SQM - 6.8 CT
Year : 2021
Status : Completed

The house is set in an old & familiar neigh- borhood where the lady of the house grew up in. Familiarity of context, shared memories, and a flowering Kanikonna tree(cassia fistula ); formed the canvas that informed the design decisions one was to take. While catering to the typical program of a 3 bedroom house, it was essential that the architecture of the house, and its afterlife would maintain, & enliven the cordiality that the scene had been offering since many years..
