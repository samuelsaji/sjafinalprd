Nameh Holiday Homes

Location : Goa
Year : 2018
Status : On going

Siolife is a gated cluster of eleven beautiful vacation homes nested in the quaint Siolim village, the rustic heartland of Goa where life slows down to witness the beauty of Goan life. As a “Row House” concept, we have tried to organise the design as an ‘L’ shaped unit with front and rear gardens. The common spaces like foyer, stair core etc, flank the abutting wall, whereas the spaces of the house extend into a courtyard with a small pool around which the house is planned

Design Process:

Optimum plot size of 12m x 20m, which is 240sqm or about 6cents each.

Entry from the long access road that abuts the length of the site.

one plot reserved for the club house.

Row house concept: we’ve tried to organize the design as an ‘L’ shaped unit-with a front and rear gardens. (with the 3m setbacks). The common spaces like foyer, stair core etc. flank the abutting wall, whereas the spaces of the house extend into the courtyard, around which the house is planned.

Program:
Ground Floor:
Car porch, Verandah (opening to front yard), Foyer, Living – Dining, Kitchen, Garden Court, Powder Room Bedroom & Toilet

First Floor:
Study, Master bedroom, (with Balcony, Toilet); Bedroom, Terrace

This being a holiday home, parties can spill over to the garden courtyard from the living –dining room, thus doubling up the usable space. The garden court can well accommodate some of the beautiful existing trees on site.

One enters into a foyer that opens right into a double height space, with the stair rising up along the shared wall. On to the opposite side, the living – dining room opens out to the garden courtyard. The kitchen, a powder room for guests and a bedroom occupy the rest of the space. We considered a bedroom on the ground floor to address an aged parent or a disabled person. The bedroom also opens to the courtyard
