Munnar Resort

Year : 2018
Status : On going

A 30 key property, common facilities, restaurant, pool, lush green cover of jungle, overlooking the tea estates and a site that rises over 70m in elevation, natural streams from the top that tumble down as a waterfall through the site

Out of respect for the land, it was decided to leave thr least possible change to the fragile ecosystem. The process started off with mapping existing water channels, swales, to make sure natural hydrology is preserved. The buildable areas were then found within the slope and in workable geologies. This helped us leave maximum forest untouched, while satisfying the program requirement

Restaurant & Common areas

Upper most part of the site with the best panorama and a relatively flat slope has all the Common facilities, Pool, Restaurant, Kitchen etc. The 3 different categories of accommodation are spread in successive lower levels
